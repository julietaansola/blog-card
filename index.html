Finding Nemo in Sharm el-sheikh

Ras Mohammed National Park

Four years ago I traveled for the first time to Egypt. At first I was not sure of going, however I liked it so much that I went the next year again. In Sharm el-Sheik there is an underwater park called Ras Mohamed, a beautiful place where people go diving, but I can't do it because I have an ear operated, however I did snorkel there for the first time, and I loved it: it was how to dive into a new world and feel like I was in the movie Finding Nemo

Egypt

Giza pyramids

One of my dreams was to get to know the pyramids of giza, because when I was a child I always read books of pharaohs and sphinxes. I did not know if one day I could go, however to my surprise I was working there for a month! It was a hard month because it was very hot, but I learned a lot about the culture of that country and especially about its food. I love to discover a country through its food!
